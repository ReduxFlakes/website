## Channels

- [BawBaw](https://www.youtube.com/@BawBawTalks) - This channel appeared on my recommendations as a surprise. It's a recent, small channel, of a guy playing Minecraft while talking about life. It's a awesome channel, with awesome content that tries (with sucess) to recreate the old vibe of YouTube. This has turned into one of my favourites channels!

- [3FS](https://www.youtube.com/@3fs) - The most popular channel on this list, you can say he's overrated but to be honest, I really like his style, probably my most favorite channel about horror (other than Markiplier).

- [Ego Dip](https://www.youtube.com/@egodipmusic) - He appeared on my feed and since then I always watch his videos, most of his content is related to making music and production, the content I love the most though is him remaking popular songs, he explains really well the production process and is just fun to watch.
